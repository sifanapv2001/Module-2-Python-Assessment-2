This is a part of Module1: End project,where we analyses dataset of employees of ABC company.

1.Employee distribution by team:
Calculated number of employees in each team.

3.Predominant age group:

4.Salary Expenditure Analysis:
Determined the salary expenditure based on team and position.

5.Correlation between age and salary
Investigated relation between age and salary.
And visualised the correlation.
#### Visualizations:
The following visualization were created:
1.Pie plot(Distribution of employees on team,Age distribution)

2.Bar chart(Position of employees,Age distribution,team and position salary relation)

4.Scatter plot(Relation between age and salary)
